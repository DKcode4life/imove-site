Step 1

Book a house survey

Choose a quick Zoom video survey or an in-person home visit. We will understand the access, volume, packing needs, and anything that needs special care.

  • Video surveys are quick and easy to book
  • Home visits are ideal for larger or more complex moves
  • We check access, parking, volume, packing, and storage needs

Step 2

Receive a clear quote

Your quote should show what is included, what is optional, and how the move will be handled. We keep the details practical so you can compare properly.

  • Clear pricing based on property size, distance, access, and services
  • Options for packing, storage, export packing, or specialist items
  • No confusing extras hidden away from the main plan

Step 3

Set the moving dates

Once you are ready, we reserve the team and vehicles. A low 10% deposit secures the moving date, then we keep the plan moving around keys, access, and timings.

  • Reserve the moving team as soon as the date starts to firm up
  • We can work around keys, chains, storage gaps, and access windows
  • Your plan stays flexible while the important date is protected
Check availability Call the team

Step 4

Prepare for moving day

Pack non-essential items early, label boxes by room, keep documents and daily essentials separate, and tell us about fragile or awkward items before the day arrives.

  • Label boxes clearly by room and priority
  • Keep documents, chargers, medication, and keys separate
  • Let us know about fragile, heavy, or awkward items before moving day
