How it works

One less job at the end of the move

End-of-tenancy cleaning can be hard to face when you are already managing packing, keys, utilities, and moving day logistics. We can help remove that pressure with a focused cleaning service tailored to move-out expectations.

It is especially useful alongside removals, rubbish clearance, and storage when the property needs to be left clear and ready.

What this means on the day

End-of-tenancy cleaning works best once the rooms are empty and final rubbish has been removed. That gives the cleaning team proper access to floors, cupboards, appliances, skirting, and corners that are usually hidden.

Pairing cleaning with removals or clearance can make the final handover feel more controlled, especially when keys and inspections are close together.
