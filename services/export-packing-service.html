How it works

Prepared for the journey ahead

Long-distance and international moves demand stronger packing standards than a simple local move. Export packing focuses on protection, stability, and organisation, reducing the risk of damage while items are in transit.

We can advise on the correct packing approach based on destination, transport method, and item type.

What this means on the day

Export packing is about protecting items for a longer, less predictable journey. We consider how items may be handled, stacked, transferred, or stored before reaching the final destination.

Using the right materials and packing method gives fragile, valuable, or sentimental items a better chance of arriving safely after international or long-distance transit.
