How it works

Declutter without losing a weekend

Moving often reveals items you no longer want to carry into the next home. We can remove bulky and awkward items quickly, helping you reduce volume and simplify the move.

Where possible, unwanted items are handled responsibly, with a focus on practical, hassle-free clearance.

What this means on the day

Clearance before a move can reduce the number of boxes, the amount of van space required, and the time spent sorting at the new property. It also helps you avoid paying to move items you no longer want.

We can work through bulky furniture, cluttered storage areas, garages, and last-minute move-out waste so the property is easier to hand over.
